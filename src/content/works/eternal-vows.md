This wedding film required a delicate balance between being present in the moment and crafting cinematic frames. The ceremony's traditional elements provided rich visual material.

## Approach

Two-camera setup with one dedicated to candid moments and one for composed shots. We used only natural light and minimal equipment to stay unobtrusive.
